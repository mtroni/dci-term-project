The Importance of Nature

Page desription


This is a wonderful website that was skillfully created to draw attention an underlooked aspect of everyday life. Nature is a natural escape, the barest form of existence. Seeing the wonders of the world outside fosters within us the urge to protect it.

Nature serves as a refuge to inspire, reflect and heal. Studies reveal that being in nature has a powerful positive effect on the mind, body, and spirit. Nature is a wonder of our world available for all.
